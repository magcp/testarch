The Rooms

The Art Room: The art room has been thoughtfully designed to provide an optimal environment for students to explore their creativity and develop their artistic skills. One key feature of the room is the presence of different spaces that cater to different aspects of the artistic process. For example, there is a dedicated art gallery within the room where students can showcase their completed works and receive feedback from their peers and instructors.

The Play Room: The playroom is an essential space that emphasizes the importance of play and self-expression for children. It serves as a valuable outlet for children to disengage from the typical classroom routine and engage in activities that allow them to explore and learn in new ways.The playroom features a diverse mix of entertainment options that cater to different types of play. Some activities are more active and physical, encouraging children to use their bodies and burn off energy, while others are more quiet and cerebral.
